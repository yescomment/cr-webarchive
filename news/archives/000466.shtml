2nd Annual City Reliquary Benefit Gala Dec. 10th, 2007

The 2nd Annual City Reliquary Benefit Gala
Will be held on Monday, December 10th 2007
Beginning at 7:00 PM
At the Automotive High School Auditorium
Located in McCarren Park, Williamsburgh, Brooklyn

Our featured guests will be,
Lawrence Weschler
Author of Mr. Wilson’s Cabinet of Wonder
Director of the New York Institute for the Humanities at NYU

Paul Lukas
ESPN.com Uni-watch columnist
And author of Inconspicuous Consumption


With musical performance by
Los Bomberos de Brooklyn
Multi-generational Puerto Rican bomba, rhythm and dance group
from Williamsburgh’s Southside


And excerpts from the unreleased film
Up on the Roof
A documentary on the rooftop pigeon keepers of Williamsburgh’s Southside

By JL Aronson producer/director of Danielson: A Family Movie


The presentation will be followed by limousine service
to a very special dinner beginning at 9:00PM at,
Bamonte’s Restaurant
Williamsburgh’s most historic Italian restaurant
Over 100 years in business with countless stories to tell and the finest in Brooklyn cuisine.


Tickets:
Auditorium and Dinner: $125 (RSVP required)





Auditorium Presentation Only: $30 (at the door)
