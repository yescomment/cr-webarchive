CR BAZAAR- Rummage Sale & BINGO: August 12th & 13th

Gathering the Goods: July 21st through August 11th, 2006.

Sale Dates: August 12th & 13th, 10:00AM
Early Bird Entrance: 8:00 - 10:00 AM with $5.00 fee


Please, DONATE what-have-you:
Start bringing all the fascinating stuff that you love so much... you'll give it away ... to the City Reliquary for our Seasonal Rummage Sale. We won't be accepting clothes, but we will be accepting most other things - records, musical instruments, tools, appliances, furniture, antiques, collectibles... etcetera, etcetera. We'll be selling these fabulous items at the Reliquary in a desperate but charming attempt to pay September's rent. You'll be gaining storage space, CR love and maybe even a tax deduction. Bring your donations to the City Reliquary at 370 Metropolitan Ave - open Thursdays and Fridays from 6-9pm and weekends from 12 - 8. Or call, 718.599.0134 (starting 8/2/6) to schedule a drop off or pick-up time.

Also, stop by for a fast and furious round or two of BINGO on the Sidewalk, complete with fabulous prizes and special Bingo Hostess. Each card costs only $2.00, comes with a marker, and will benefit the CR Museum. Games begin at 12:00 high noon.
