Bicycle Fetish Day Participants Wanted

You are invited to participate in, New York City’s only… BICYCLE FETISH DAY Saturday, May 28th starting at 12:00 noon Hosted by The Brooklyn Civic Riders Bicycle Club Located at The City Reliquary Museum, in Williamsburg, Brooklyn On Havemeyer St. between Grand and Hope Sts. In typical car show vein, cyclists from all genres will show off their pride & joy at this special event. There will be representatives from all types of cycling clubs including; track bikes, vintage & chromed out, downhill & fat tires, street bikes, choppers & low riders, tall bikes, and custom rides of all types. The street will be closed to cars and open to an impressive line-up of all kinds of bicycles. If you love to ride, and you love your ride, this is your day. Come celebrate the diversity of our NYC bicycle culture. We need you!! Bicycle Shop Owners, Bicycle Club Leaders, Food Vendors, Friends & Neighbors, Bicycle Collectors, Bicycle Advocates, Civic Organizations of all types, we are looking for you to participate in the following ways: Host or Judge a Bicycle Contest (wheelies, track stands, best restoration, best in show, etc.), Set up a booth to disseminate your materials and info, Set up a table to show off your collection, Bring your club members and meet other enthusiasts , Host a bicycle demonstration (unicycles, bmx tricks, etc.), Please contact Bill Scanga at civicriders@cityreliquary.org or call 917.373.7909 City Reliquary Museum P.O. Box 110-948 Brooklyn, NY 11211 THANK YOU and as always, RIDE CIVIC!!
