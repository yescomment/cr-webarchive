Feeding Desire - Inspector Collector

10 ice cream spoons and paddles from my collection are on display at the Smithsonian’s Cooper Hewitt National Design Museum in a show called Feeding Desire (about tools of the table from 1500 til today). Can you believe the kind of colorful plastic and wooden spoons you get free with ice cream are sharing cases with Tiffany sterling silver ice cream saws, Napoleon’s travel service and George Washington’s forks? Check em out at 5th Avenue and 91st Street in Manhattan until October 29, 2006.
