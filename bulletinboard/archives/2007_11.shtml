The 2nd Annual City Reliquary Benefit Gala

THE 2ND ANNUAL CITY RELIQUARY BENEFIT GALA
MONDAY, DECEMBER 10, 2007, BEGINNING AT 7 PM
A PRESENTATION AND GALA DINNER
The Presentation will be held at the Automotive High School Auditorium
Located in McCarren Park, Williamsburgh, Brooklyn

Featured Guest: LAWRENCE WESCHLER, Author of "Mr. Wilson's Cabinet of Wonder", Director of the New York Institute for the Humanities at NYU

Brooklyn Dodgers Presentation: PAUL LUKAS, ESPN.Com Uni Watch Columnist
& Author of "Inconspicuous Consumption"

Musical Performance:LOS BOMBEROS DE BROOKLYN, multi-generational Puerto Rican Bomba, Rhythm and Dance Group from Williamsburgh's Southside

And Excerpts from the unreleased film: UP ON THE ROOF a documentary on the rooftop pigeon keepers of Williamsburgh's Southside
by JL ARONSON, Producer/Director of "Danielson: A Family Movie"

The Presentation will be followed by Limousine Service
to a VERY SPECIAL DINNER Beginning at 9:00 PM at BAMONTE'S RESTAURANT, Williamsburgh's Most Historic Italian Restaurant

TICKETS: Auditorium Presentation only: $30 (At the Door)
Auditorium and Dinner: $125 (RSVP Required)
