Truckers Atlas

A Compilation Taken from The Road Tapes of Two Art Handlers Dave Herman, President and Jesse James Arnold, Web and Music Tech, spent six months traveling across this fine country of ours delivering famous, heavier than you would believe artworks, to both famous places you've never heard of, and to the souless rich.

In the midst of their travels one of the only things that kept them sane and kept them truckin were some of these songs. Through ice and snow, heavy rain and roadkill ( as well as the the vacant nothing that is South Dakota ), this was their soundtrack.
