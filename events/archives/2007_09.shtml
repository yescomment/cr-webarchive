Tribute to Our City 2007

Saturday September 8th, 2007 12-6PM The fourth annual Tribue to Our City will be held on Havemeyer Street between Grand & Hope Sts. A neighborhood pot-luck picnic in the streets complete with live music, games, entertainment, free food and good will all day long! Please come out to join us.
