Tribute 017

The City Reliquary was honored to have The Circus Contraption as special guest performers at the September Tribute. They are visiting New York from Seattle through October 1st. Make it a point not to miss their amazing show!! Here is the info...

Thursday September 1 through Saturday October 1, 2005
Wednesdays through Sundays
Theater for the New City
155 1st Avenue (between East 9th and East 10th Streets)
Tickets: $20 adults / $15 students and seniors (with ID)
Opening Weekend (Sept 1-4) and all Wednesdays are pay-what-you-can
